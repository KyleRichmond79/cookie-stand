Pat's salmon cookies were originally entered into a cooking competition for the 2013 Ballard Locks "Salmon SEEson" opener. Pat took first place and was given such high praise for her salmon cookie she decided to turn it into business.

Pat's first Cookie Stand opened up in Pike's Place Market in 2014. There are now 5 stores and looking to expand. If you are interested in helping Pat find new locations please feel free to email her.

Pat is devoted to giving back to the local community. Currently she is donating 15% of all her sales to to charity and you can help us do that by stopping by any of our locations and getting yourself, or anyone, some of our delicious cookies!
